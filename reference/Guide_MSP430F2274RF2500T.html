Guide to the MSP430Based F2274 2.4-GHz Wireless Target Board (EZ430-RF2500T)

Pin numbering for the RF2500T variant in Energia follows the pin numbering of the header on the target board (see pin mapping diagram below for details). Example: Header pin P3 can be turned on with: 

 pinMode(3, OUTPUT);
 digitalWrite(3, HIGH);

Pins can also be addressed by their GPIO peripheral names P1_x, P2_x, P3_x and P4_x. See Energia/hardware/msp430/variants/ez430-rf2500t/pins_energia.h for details. The RF2500T pins_energia.h is here on github. 

 pinMode(P2_0, OUTPUT);
 digitalWrite(P2_0, HIGH);

LED1, LED2, S1 and internal temp sensor can also be addressed by the alternative names: 

 RED_LED
 GREEN_LED;
 PUSH1;
 TEMPSENSOR;

To turn on the red LED. 

 pinMode(RED_LED, OUTPUT);
 digitalWrite(RED_LED, HIGH);
